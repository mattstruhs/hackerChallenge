What makes a "superfood" SUPER?

Super-foods are certain fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds touted for their superior health benefits. Compared with other foods, they have higher amounts of certain vitamins and minerals and powerful antioxidants. They often are associated with combating high blood pressure, diabetes, heart disease, and even cancer.
